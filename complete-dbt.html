Overview of DBT

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) is an evidence-based approach to emotional regulation, stress tolerance, mindfulness, and interpersonal effectiveness. It helps you navigate life's challenges with clarity and confidence.

Key Takeaway

DBT combines acceptance and change strategies to help you create a life worth living by building skills in four key areas that work together as an integrated whole.
