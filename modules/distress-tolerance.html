Distress Tolerance

Learn to manage intense emotions and challenging situations with effective DBT skills.

Overview of Distress Tolerance

Distress Tolerance skills help you navigate difficult situations and emotions without making them worse. These skills focus on managing crises, accepting reality, and calming yourself.

Key Takeaway

Learning to tolerate distress builds resilience and helps break the cycle of impulsive reactions that often make difficult situations worse.
