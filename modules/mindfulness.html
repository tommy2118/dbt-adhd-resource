Mindfulness

Develop present-moment awareness with DBT mindfulness skills.

Overview of Mindfulness

Mindfulness is a foundational DBT skill that helps you stay present and aware of your thoughts, emotions, and surroundings. It promotes nonjudgmental observation and effective participation in the present moment.

Key Takeaway

Mindfulness creates space between your emotions and reactions, allowing you to respond thoughtfully rather than react automatically.
