Emotion Regulation

Learn to understand, manage, and shift emotions effectively with DBT skills.

Overview of Emotion Regulation

Emotion Regulation skills teach you how to understand your emotions, reduce emotional vulnerability, and shift overwhelming emotions to promote well-being and effective decision-making.

Key Takeaway

Emotions provide valuable information, but they don't have to control your actions. Learning to regulate emotions gives you more choices in how you respond to situations.
